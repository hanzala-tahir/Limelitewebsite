Our Story

Born in January 2020, Limelite started with a single, electrifying mission: to create an innovative product that empowers small business owners and self-employed individuals to reach a wider audience and keep their brands thriving.

Our advertising backpacks emerged as a game-changer, delivering extraordinary value by providing a cost-effective way to capture attention and build brand awareness.

  • The Insight: A portable, eco-friendly advertising solution that shines bright in any setting.
  • Sustainability: Committed to a circular system and fully offsetting our carbon emissions.
  • Our Mission: Not just creating a product, but striving to create a better future for all.
